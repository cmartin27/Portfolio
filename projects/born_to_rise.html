Born To Rise

Racoons Make Games

This represents the culminating project for my HND in Videogames Programming at ESAT. It involves the collaborative development of a 2.5D platformer undertaken by a diverse team consisting of 7 programmers, 6 artists, and 3 game designers. To check the game's evolution, you can follow its progress at @BornToRiseGame.

Our project is designed to emulate the dynamics of a professional video game studio closely. Operating under tight schedules marked by deadlines and crucial milestones, such as the prototype and first playable versions, we adhere to an adapted version of the scrum methodology. Weekly sprints define our work cycles, commencing and concluding with meetings that evaluate the progress of each department and gauge our alignment with project objectives.

Within the realm of this action-centric 2.5D platformer, we've curated an immersive experience characterized by a dark and atmospheric ambiance. I took charge of the core mechanics and animations of the player. Additionally, my role extended to crafting the camera system that seamlessly navigates the player through the intricacies of our virtual world. Finally, I undertook the crucial responsibility of coordinating the programming team. This involved fostering cohesion among team members and other disciplines ensuring that our collective efforts harmonized to bring our vision to life.

The repository of this project is private but code snippets may be given to anyone interested in its implementation. Contact me for more information.
