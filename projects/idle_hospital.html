Idle Pixel Hospital

February, 2022 - May, 2022

Unity, C#, Android

Idle game where you manage a hospital. Developed for Android and available in Google Play.

Made By

Carmen Martín Moreno

Mario Rodríguez Chaves

Ramón Pérez Segarra

Idle Pixel Hospital is a captivating tycoon game developed by the fictional studio MaracaSoft, in collaboration with my colleagues Mario Rodríguez Chaves and Ramón Pérez Segarra. The game is available on Google Play for Android devices. In this game, you take on the role of managing a hospital with the primary objectives of maximizing wealth and attracting a multitude of patients. As you successfully attend to more patients, your revenue increases, enabling you to improve hospital services and eventually transition to larger medical institutions with higher taxes for your patients. Idle Pixel Hospital offers an engaging simulation where financial success and the expansion of your healthcare empire are intricately intertwined.

What I did

  • UI Design and Implementation
  • Calculation and display of general statistics
  • Music and Sound Effects integration
  • ASO for Google Play and App Store
  • Game Design and Monetization Study

Game Features

  • Simulation of a realistic economic model
  • Fully functional advertisement using rewarded ads.
  • Prototype of an in-game store.
  • Released on the Google Play Store.
  • ASO study for Google Play and App Store.
